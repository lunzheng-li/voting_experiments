Instructions

There are {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants in today's experiment. You will participate two paid sessions of 3 rounds each.

In each round of the experiment, You will be randomly assigned to one of two groups the ALPHA group or the BETA group (Note that you will not necessarily be in the same group during each round).

  • In Session 1 (round 1 to {{ Constants.end_s1_round }}), the ALPHA group has {{ Constants.N_A }} members and the BETA group has {{ Constants.N_B }} members;
  • In Session 2 (round {{ Constants.start_s2_round }} to {{ Constants.num_rounds }}), the ALPHA group has {{ Constants.N_A_s2 }} members and the BETA group has {{ Constants.N_B_s2 }} members.
You will need to choose between X and Y. Your earnings depend on:
  1. Your choice - If your choice is Y, you will get a Y-bonus;
  2. Which group has the most members choosing X.
See the following "Y-bonus" and "Your Earning" sections for details regarding your payment.

Y-bonus

The amount of your Y-bonus is assigned randomly by the computer. In any given round you have an equal chance of being assigned any Y-bonus between 0 and 55 points.

Your Y-bonus in each round does not depend on your Y-bonus or decisions in previous rounds, or on the Y-bonuses and decisions of other participants. Since Y-bonuses are assigned separately for each participant, different participants will typically have different Y-bonuses.

While you are told your own Y-bonus, you are never told the Y-bonus of other participants. You only know that each of the other participants has a Y-bonus that is some number between 0 and 55.

Your Earning

Your earnings are computed in the following way. Suppose you choose X:

  • If your group has more members choosing X than the other group, then you will earn 105 points;
  • If both groups have the same number of members choosing X, then you will earn 55 points;
  • If the other group has more members choosing X than your group, then you will earn 5 points.
Alternatively, suppose you choose Y:
  • If your group has more members choosing X than the other group, then you will earn 105 points plus your Y-bonus;
  • If both groups have the same number of members choosing X, then you will earn 55 points plus your Y-bonus;
  • If the other group has more members choosing X than your group, then you will earn 5 points plus your Y-bonus.
At the end of each round, you will see a "Result" screen summarizing the amount that you earned in the round.

At the end of the last paid session, you will be paid the sum of what you have earned in the two paid sessions, plus the show-up fee of $5.00. Everyone will be paid in private, and you are under no obligation to tell others how much you earned. Your earnings during the experiment are denominated in POINTS. Your DOLLAR earnings are determined by multiplying your earnings in POINTS by a conversion rate. In this experiment, the conversion rate is 0.0037, meaning that 100 POINTS is worth 37 cents.
